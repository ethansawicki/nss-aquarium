Lion Fish

Lion Fish Pricing:

Anywhere from $15 to $110.

Lion Fish Harvest:

Native to Indo Pacific and South Pacific

Lion Fish Care:

Water Temps 74°F to 80°F. pH levels 8.1 - 8.4. Feed 1-2 times daily. Provide rock and decor for hiding places

Clown Fish

Clown Fish Pricing:

Anywhere from $15 to $25. Larger ones can cost up to $100.

Clown Fish Harvesting locations:

Maldives, Australia, Hawaii, Thailand

Clown Fish Care:

Water Temps 72°F to 78°F. Min. 20 Gallon tank. Add marine salt to water. Recreate daylight cycle. Provide rock and decor for hiding places.
